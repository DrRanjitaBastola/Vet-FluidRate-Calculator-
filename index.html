Shock Fluid Resuscitation

Shock Dose: Dogs: 90 ml/kg | Cats: 40 ml/kg
Administration: Give over 15-30 minutes, reassess, repeat as needed
Always reassess patient after each shock dose administration. Monitor for fluid overload. It is NOT Recemmended to give full shock dose at once. Microboluses are for the win!
